How are you?

To everyone who visits the homepage of Jeollanam-do Fire Department, we sincerely welcome you! Jeollanam-do is an area where the natural environment coexists alongside basic industrial facilities, and there are various demands for firefighting required by the aging agricultural and fishing villages and the centralization of new cities.

Accordingly, we are planning to build a fire safety net by developing firefighting power relocation and response tactics suited to local characteristics in order to protect the lives and property of residents.

In addition, we will fulfill our role and responsibilities as a 119 service provider in protecting the safety of residents.

We ask for advice and encouragement from all netizens for the development of the Jeonnam firefighting administration, and we aim to provide useful and diverse information through our website.
Thank you!

head of the fire department in Jeollanam-do

Ma Jae-yoon
