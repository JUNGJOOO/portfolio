Discover our all-inclusive services below,

MASK (Matching Assistance Service by KITA)
  • Step 1 GBMS
  • Step 2 K-BTS
  • Step 3 VCS

01

GBMS Program

(Global Business Matching Service)

  • · Experts of each product category assist finding
    the most suitable Korean suppliers
  • · Able to receive Korean potential supplier list
    only within two business days.

02

K-BTS Services

(KITA Baton Touch Service)

  • · KITA Staff can meet the Korean company
    in which the buyer is interested on behalf of Buyer.
  • · Based on buyer’s Questionnaire, we will meet &
    visit Korean companies’ Office and Factory.

03

VCS Program

(Video Calls Service)

  • · 1:1 Video meeting support to discuss
    both companies and products details.

When the business environment recovers from the COVID-19 outbreak,
we will resume supporting you by VIB service, which assists offline meetings with you and Korean suppliers.
